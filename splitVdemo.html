    heights: [50, 50],

Vertical Example 2: A horizontal split with three elements, starting with even widths with 100px, 100px and 300px minimum widths, respectively.

The first pane of the horizontal split is itself split vertically into two sub-panes, starting with even heights and with 50px, 100px minimum heights, respectively.


Split(['v2-one', 'v2-two', 'v2-three'], {
    minWidth: [100, 100, 300]
});
SplitV(['v2-one-top', 'v2-one-bottom'], {
    minHeight: [50, 100]
});
